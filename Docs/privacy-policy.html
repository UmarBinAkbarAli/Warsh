Privacy Policy

App Name: Warsh
Package: com.warsh.app
Last updated: May 29, 2026

1. Introduction

Warsh ("we", "our", or "us") is an AI-powered Arabic language learning application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Warsh mobile app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you register and use the app:

  • Account information: your name and email address when you register
  • Learning progress: lessons completed, XP earned, streaks, and quiz results
  • Chat messages: conversations with our AI tutor (Ustaad Noor) to provide personalised responses
  • Device information: basic device data for crash reporting and performance monitoring

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the learning experience
  • Track your progress and maintain your streak
  • Power the AI tutor (Ustaad Noor) using OpenAI's API
  • Send optional push notifications for streak reminders
  • Monitor app performance and fix bugs (via Sentry)

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • OpenAI – AI tutor responses (chat messages are sent to OpenAI's API). See OpenAI Privacy Policy.
  • Sentry – error monitoring and crash reporting. See Sentry Privacy Policy.
  • Neon (PostgreSQL) – cloud database hosting your account and progress data.
  • Google Play – app distribution and in-app purchase processing.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS) and implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and learning data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

Warsh is designed for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time via your device settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
